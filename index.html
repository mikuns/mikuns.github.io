Hi, am Samuel Akinseinde, a techy fellow passionate about leveraging technology for health care.

I work as a software engineer for FINT Technology Africa Limited empowering the financial stability of Nigerians, and also, as a research affiliate with Covenant University’s IoT-Enabled Smart and Connected Communities (SmartCU) Research Cluster with a research focus on smart tools for maternal and child health care. My research interest centers around smart tools and machine intelligence for health care.

This website includes my educational background, professional experiences, expertise, and some of my latest updates. I hope it enlightens you more about me.
