MedPair (with Bright Abhulimen) - Sandoz HACk Healthcare Access Challenge 2018

In Nigeria, access to health care professionals relative to specific need is a difficult pathway. We identified 101 requests for a few health care professionals (Urologist, Therapist, Gynecologist, Psychiatrist and Dermatologist) made by Nigerians just on Twitter from January 1st 2017 to November 26th 2018. 52% of the requests were attributed to mental health (Therapist/Psychiatrist/Shrink) which aligns with World Bank's eMBeD findings in 2016 that 1 in 5 Nigerians have depressive symptoms. Also, we discussed with a few health specialists and found out that apart from inadequate access to these professionals, a lot of Nigerians are ignorant of who to approach when faced with health issues. Health care Professionals need to be easily accessible to potential clients especially those afflicted with health issues that call for immediate response.

MedPair is a mobile platform that aims to easily connect clients with health professionals relative to specific needs.
